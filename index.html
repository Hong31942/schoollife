Activities

NFLSBCA is always famous for its various activities, and I actually experienced and felt that in high school.

I used to be very quiet and shy, but I have changed in some ways when taking the activities in BCA.

In Grade 10, I tried to take as many clubs as some Grade 12 students suggested, but then I found my weak personality - I cannot overcome my SHYNESS and FEAR when communicating with others.

But luckily, there are guys that treated me very well in clubs and I really wanted to thank them for taking care of me. They are the ones that first contributed to the changes that always continued in these years.

I will also thank the BCA teachers for helping me. They are not only teachers but wise and knowledgeable friends.

Then, in Grade 11 and Grade 12, I did a lot of things I never thought of...

Like running for the Students' Council... Like being the leader of one club...

University Life - King's College London

King's College London is a world-famous university with a long history. There are beautiful buildings everywhere on its campuses, so walking through King's always makes me feel the flying time.

University life is significantly different from high school life. Students are more independent with their own plans. Everybody is working hard towards their purposes.

In King's, I also met many schoolmates from different majors and countries. We discussed about our study and life.

After class, we also cooked together, travelled together and had parties together, it was all beautiful memories.
